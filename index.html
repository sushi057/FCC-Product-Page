  • "Echoes" is a song by the rock band Pink Floyd, and the sixth and last track from their 1971 album Meddle. It is 23 and a half minutes long and takes up the entire second side of the original LP, making it the single longest song the band produced debuting at No. 2 on Billboard Hot 100.

  • The track evolved from variety of different musical themes and ideas, including instrumental passages and studio effects, resulting in the side-long piece with themes of human communication and empathy.

  • Echoes" has been regarded by critics as an important song that transitions between Pink Floyd's early experimental material and later mainstream success and a personal favourite of mine.
